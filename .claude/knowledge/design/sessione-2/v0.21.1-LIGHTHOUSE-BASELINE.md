**Stato applicato sul droplet `saltelli-staging-ams3-01`:**

- **Backup pre-modify**: `/etc/nginx/sites-available/saltelli.v0.21.0.backup` (root-owned, 1531 byte).

- **Cache rules** (location regex precede `location /`):
  | Pattern | TTL | Cache-Control |

  | `*.jpg \| *.jpeg \| *.png \| *.gif \| *.ico \| *.svg` | 6mo | `public, max-age=15552000` |
- **Security headers** (server-level + duplicati nei location con cache-control):
  - `X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff`
  - `X-Frame-Options: SAMEORIGIN`
  - `Referrer-Policy: strict-origin-when-cross-origin`
  - `Permissions-Policy: geolocation=(), microphone=(), camera=()`

#### Top issues identificate (da Lighthouse audit details)

**Critical (Performance):**
1. **Render blocking requests** — savings 1570ms su /contatti/, ~650ms su /. Suggerisce critical CSS inline o async-load di sections.css (190KB).
2. **Reduce unused CSS** — 29 KiB unused. Sections.css contiene CSS per tutti i template; ogni page usa ~30%.
3. **Reduce unused JavaScript** — 20-21 KiB. GSAP/Lenis caricano sempre; alcuni template non li usano (es. /contatti/ statico).
4. **Modern HTTP** — savings 650-760ms. Nginx serve HTTP/1.1 default su staging; HTTP/2 richiede nginx config update (`listen 443 ssl http2;`).
5. **Browser errors were logged to the console** — da indagare manualmente in DevTools (probabile CF7 o Brevo endpoint fail).

**Critical (A11y):**
1. **Background and foreground colors do not have a sufficient contrast ratio** — alcune coppie (probabile `--text-muted` su `--surface` o cream).
2. **Elements with visible text labels do not have matching accessible names** — link/button con aria-label che diverge dal testo visibile.
3. **Touch targets do not have sufficient size or spacing** (mobile only) — tap targets <48×48 in qualche elemento (probabile footer link).

**Critical (SEO):**
1. **Page is blocked from indexing** — `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` o `X-Robots-Tag` server header. **INTENZIONALE su staging**, removed in produzione cut.
2. **Document does not have a meta description** — Yoast genera fallback ma alcune pagine (es. /casi/, /404/) non hanno descrizione esplicita.
